Welcome to SuperX 5.0 "Lamarr"

Welcome and thank you for choosing SuperX 5.0 "Lamarr", as your system is being installed, this slideshow will show you some of the functions and features of the system.

Browse the web with ease and safety

Firefox is the default browser of SuperX, used by millions of people around the globe and known for its openness, speed, and reliability. You are assured to be on the safe side with Firefox and browse your favorite websites like Facebook, Gmail, etc. with ease and confidence.

Featured software

  • Firefox web browser

  • Chromium

  • KTorrent

Enjoy multimedia with ease

SuperX comes pre-installed with VLC Media Player, enabling you to play almost all formats without any additional codec installation.


For your music collection, SuperX includes the Musique audio player, a simple and intuitive music player. Kdenlive video editor is also included by default for those your amateur video editing needs.

Featured software

  • VLC Media Player

  • Musique Player

  • Kdenlive Video Editor

Create documents and write in your own language

SuperX comes with LibreOffice, enabling you to create and edit documents, spreadsheets, and presentations that are compatible with Microsoft Office.


SuperX also supports multilingual text input for many scripts used across the world out of the box.

Featured software

  • LibreOffice Writer

  • LibreOffice Calc

  • LibreOffice Impress

Find even more software

With SuperX 5.0 "Lamarr" we are introducing the SuperX AppStore - A convenient place to browse, install and update your applications.


While still in active development and a work-in-progress, we have included the store with Lamarr for getting feedback and improve upon.

Simplicity with customization

At the core of the SuperX 5.0 user interface is the KDE Plasma 5, which is known for giving power to the user while being simple by default.


With SuperX we have tried to further enhance the user experience by making Plasma 5 even simpler to use and made sure you can customize every aspect of your desktop. Choose from various themes, and desktop widgets and create your own unique style.

Works with your hardware

A wide range of hardware support comes built-in with SuperX, ready to plug and play.


For anything not included built-in, SuperX comes with the Driver Manager, which lets you install proprietary drivers and other drivers not included in SuperX by default.

Security by design

Security comes built in with SuperX. SuperX is immune to common viruses and malware. SuperX by design respects your privacy and does not collect any private information.


With Plasma Vault built-in, you can securely store your sensitive data encrypted and secured.

Featured software

  • Plasma Vault

  • AppArmor

  • Nx Firewall
